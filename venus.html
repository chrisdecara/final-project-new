Venus: A Fiery Mess

Ah, Venus! To many, Venus is nothing more than a massive ball of fire that rests in the night sky, reminding us all to turn our intergalactic ovens off at night. But to some, Venus is more: it's also a death trap.

Yes, to the average alien like myself, Venus is somewhat of a problem. Its magnetic center is aligned in a way that perfectly obstructs navigational equpimnet onboard spacecraft, often causing personnel to panic and steer the ship directly into the planet's surface. It's... not ideal.
